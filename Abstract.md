This project applies Exploratory Data Analysis and Machine Learning 
on the Heart Failure Prediction dataset which contains 918 patient 
records with 11 clinical features such as age, cholesterol, blood 
pressure and chest pain type. Three models were trained and evaluated — 
Decision Tree, Logistic Regression and Naive Bayes. Logistic Regression 
achieved the highest accuracy of 87% making it the best performer for 
this dataset. The key finding is that chest pain type, maximum heart 
rate and ST slope are the strongest predictors of heart disease.
